Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How does Nirikshak integrate with existing CCTV systems?

Nirikshak connects to your existing CCTV infrastructure through standard video protocols (RTSP, ONVIF). No new cameras or hardware installation is required in most cases.

What is the detection accuracy?

Our AI models achieve 99%+ accuracy in detecting PPE violations under normal lighting conditions. The system continuously learns and improves over time.

How quickly are alerts sent?

Alerts are sent within 1 second of violation detection, ensuring immediate notification for quick corrective action.

Is the data stored securely?

Yes, all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with industry-standard security protocols and offer both cloud and on-premise deployment options.

What industries can benefit from Nirikshak?

Nirikshak is designed for any hygiene-critical environment including commercial kitchens, hospitals, food processing plants, pharmaceutical facilities, and manufacturing units.
