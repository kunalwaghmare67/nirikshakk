About Nirikshak

Intelligent hygiene monitoring powered by AI and computer vision

The Problem

In environments like kitchens, hospitals, and food processing units, maintaining hygiene is critical but difficult to monitor continuously.

Manual monitoring is inconsistent and prone to human error

24/7 surveillance is expensive and resource-intensive

Violations often go unnoticed until it's too late

No historical data for compliance audits and improvements

Hygiene Violations Cost Lives

Foodborne illnesses, hospital infections, and contamination incidents can be prevented with proper monitoring

Automated Intelligence

Nirikshak transforms your existing CCTV into an intelligent monitoring system

The Solution

Nirikshak is an intelligent hygiene monitoring system that uses existing CCTV cameras and AI-based object detection to ensure compliance with safety protocols.

Leverages existing CCTV infrastructure - no new hardware needed

AI-powered detection of PPE and hygiene violations

Real-time alerts for immediate corrective action

Historical data storage for audits and trend analysis
